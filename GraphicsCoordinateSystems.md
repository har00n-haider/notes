
### Notes

Some of these environments also have a concept of forward, right and up. Here are some of those conventions:

|         | forward | up | right |
|---------|---------|----|-------|
| blender | +y      | +z | +x    |
| unity   | +z      | +y | +x    |
| unreal  | +x      | +z | +y    |
